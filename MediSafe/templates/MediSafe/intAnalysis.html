    HIGH SEVERITY
    MECHANISM & INTERACTION

    Concurrent use increases the risk of serious bleeding complications. Aspirin inhibits platelet aggregation while Warafarin inhibits clotting factor synthesis.

    CLINICAL RECOMMENDATION

    Avoid this combination if possible. If required, monitor INR more frequently and assess for signs of occult bleeding (bruising, melena). Consider alternative analgesics like Acetaminophen.
